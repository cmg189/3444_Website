This is how you play!

Food Fight is a single player game based on the popular Battle Ship game! In Food Fight the objective is to eat all the opponents food before they eat yours. When starting the game you will be presented with a 10 x 10 grid where your food items are held. Click the Randomize Food Items button until you find a configuation you like.

Food items include:

Bread 1x3
Pizza 3x3
Donut 2x2
Pocky 1x3
Bento Box 2x3

Once you click the Ready button your food items will be shifted to the right hand side and you will be presented with an empty grid. This is the computers grid and their food items are hidden in random positions!

You will take turns trying to eat food items by simply clicking on grid cell with you cursor. Locating a food item will be indcated by a circle and a cell not contating a food item will be indicated by an X

You can restart the game by clicking the Restart button at any time.

The first person to eat all the other player's food wins!

Good luck and get to eating!
